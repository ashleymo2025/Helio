Meet the Team 

Helio was founded by 2 curious and determined teens, working with hope towards a brighter future; one that brings the lupus misdiagnosis rate from 50 to 0% so that the other 5+ million people in the world can receive the treatment they deserve. We decided to take a completely different approach than the status quo, taking advantage of the power of emerging technologies to tackle big problems in the medical diagnostic field. 

Ashley Mo
Co-founder

"Google says only 5 million in the world are diagnosed with lupus. But with misdiagnosis rates above 50%, we know that number is much higher than anyone would think. With this, we figured something had to be done.

Isabelle Lau
Co-founder

“If we want to take the misdiagnosis rate of lupus from 50% to 0% we can’t be using the same methods and processes. We’re taking breakthrough technology and turning it into a solution that is out of this world, to solve a huge problem.”
