The Innovators Behind Helio

Helio was founded by two curious and determined teens, working with hope towards building easier lives for lupus warriors. We realized the status quo just wasn't enough to give lupus patients the care they deserve, so we're applying the power of emerging technologies to change this.

If we want to give lupus patients better lives, we can't be using the same methods as processes as we do now. We're taking breakthrough technology and turning it into a solution that is out of this world, to solve a huge problem.
